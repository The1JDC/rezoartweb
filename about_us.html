About Us

Backed by a wealth of experience at technology leadership positions, we view technology as the foundation for product innovation. We feel the key differientator for technologists is the ability to actively contribute to the product (visioneering).

To be able to accomplish this, we strive to always answer the basic 'what' and 'why' questions before tackling the how. The fundamental creation of a product feature or product line starts with the "5 whys" questions. We then formulate an hypothesis that we proceed to test using product development techniques such as "dual track agile". For instance, when developing a chatbot, iterate on features as frequently as possible validating against hypothesis and metrics.

What We Do

Software Architecture

Top down and bottom-up assessment of existing architectures. Green-field and brown-field recommendations of architecture solutions. Deep-dives into architecture paint points. Strategic technology recommendation for long term planning.

Chatbots Development

End to end solutions recommendations and chatbot development (Lexical solutions). Possibility to combine with Machine Learning options.

AI: Machine Learning and Deep Learning

Advice on data pipeline and architecture to support AI-level data inflow. Hypothesis testing and feature development around unsupervised learning options. Evaluation of automation and migration of existing supervised learning (and the wisdom to move to unsupervised learning - There is no one size fits all-).

Identity Graph Execution Consulting

Provide expertise on identity graph and PII related topics such as onboarding users from offline CRM to online databases.

Technology Management Consulting

Provide advice on strategic technology decision (i.e: cloud/hybrid/multi-cloud...) and provide technology updates on latest developments.
